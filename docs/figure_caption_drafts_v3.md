## Figure 3

Figure 3 | Centerpiece: pre-commit backbone principle and thermodynamic branch reordering. The confirmatory ridge is interpreted through a pre-commit backbone in which delay acts as an admissibility condition, memory acts as productive smoothing rather than stale guidance, and flow orders which branch of the ridge is favored. Matched mechanism diagnostics show that the competitive structure is already created before final crossing through first-commit timing, wall dwell before commitment, and recycling burden. Under the thermodynamic upgrade, the ridge survives but the preferred efficient branch shifts from the moderate-flow `eta_sigma` family toward the high-flow `eta_completion_drag` branch, while trap-aware refinement does not overturn that shift. The figure therefore centers the paper on a geometry-tested pre-commit principle rather than on one optimization metric or one geometry-specific optimum.

## Figure 4

Figure 4 | Geometry-tested universality with coefficient-level renormalization. The first geometry-transfer validation shows that the pre-commit backbone ordering survives across the tested family `GF0`, `GF1`, and `GF2`, even though geometric scales, local encounter baselines, and effective coefficients renormalize. What transfers robustly is the shape-level organization of the backbone and the qualitative roles of delay, memory, and flow; what does not yet transfer as a universal law is the full crossing-completion layer or a coefficient-exact collapse. This figure is the main evidence that the project has moved beyond one gated maze while remaining conservative about scope.

## General Caption Policy

Use the following hierarchy consistently across all captions:

- strongest current claim: geometry-tested, thermodynamically upgraded pre-commit transport principle
- strongest safe claim: shape-level universality with coefficient-level renormalization
- outside scope: full crossing law, full thermodynamic closure, coefficient-exact universality

Avoid caption language that implies:

- a universal full crossing law
- total entropy production has been computed
- one metric gives the final efficiency truth
- one geometry alone establishes the whole principle
