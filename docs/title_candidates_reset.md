# Title Candidates Reset

## Purpose

These title candidates are designed for the new broad-interest storyline: the paper is about when transport performance is selected in constrained non-equilibrium systems, not about optimization in one gated maze.

Scope rule:

- the boldest titles should be paired with an abstract that keeps the qualifiers `across the tested geometry family` and `within current bookkeeping`
- none of these titles should be read as claiming a full crossing-completion law, unrestricted generality, coefficient-exact collapse, or full thermodynamic closure

## Title Candidates

1. **Selection Before Crossing in Constrained Non-equilibrium Transport**
   - slightly more `Nature`-like; broad, direct, and centered on the core question

2. **Transport Performance Is Selected Before Final Crossing**
   - broadest and most memorable; strongest if paired with an explicitly scoped abstract

3. **Pre-commit Organization Selects Transport Performance Before Crossing**
   - emphasizes mechanism without sounding like a workflow paper

4. **A Pareto-like Ridge Reveals Selection Before Crossing in Constrained Transport**
   - best if the paper wants the structural discovery in the title itself

5. **A Pre-commit Backbone Organizes Transport Tradeoffs Before Final Crossing**
   - slightly more `Nature Physics`-like; names the main physical object

6. **Reliability, Speed and Cost Are Selected Before Crossing in Constrained Transport**
   - foregrounds the three-way tradeoff for broad readers

7. **Pre-commit Selection Creates a Pareto-like Ridge in Constrained Transport**
   - compact and discovery-driven

8. **A Delay-admissible Pre-commit Backbone Organizes Transport Tradeoffs**
   - more physics-forward; stronger on mechanism than on the broad problem

9. **Geometry-tested Selection Before Crossing in Delayed-memory Transport**
   - safest title candidate; carries scope more explicitly

10. **A Geometry-tested Pre-commit Backbone for Constrained Transport Tradeoffs**
    - slightly more `Nature Physics`-like and especially safe on scope

## Recommended Front-runners

If the paper wants the broadest possible framing:

- **Selection Before Crossing in Constrained Non-equilibrium Transport**

If the paper wants the clearest discovery-driven framing:

- **A Pareto-like Ridge Reveals Selection Before Crossing in Constrained Transport**

If the paper wants the safest mechanism-first framing:

- **A Pre-commit Backbone Organizes Transport Tradeoffs Before Final Crossing**
